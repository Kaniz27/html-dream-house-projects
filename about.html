About

Md. Ripon Majumder

Managing Director

Uniflex Limited, one of the leading conglomerates in Bangladesh, started its journey in 2023, consisting of real estate businesses, and has become popular & trustworthy among its customers.

Planning the smooth completion of an independent Green Zone, VIP zone and satellite town equipped with all the latest facilities, it has deployed a top team of highly qualified Architects and engineers to meet the deadline of every project.

Eight Latest-Facilitated-Model-Town have been mapped on the outskirts of Dhaka city considering the preferences & desires of dwellers, and those locations are strategically proven very lucrative for everyone.

By travelling a long way, the group has secured its summit position by experiencing different activities worthy of mentioning. It has religiously followed the client’s needs and utilized the lessons gathered in the previous projects. Moreover, innovation & partnership constantly strengthen the implementation process.

Uniflex Limited, through significant investment in all key sectors, has tremendously & meaningfully contributed to the country’s economic stability and employment crisis.

Our Mission

Delightful customer satisfaction by maximizing innovative solutions, highly motivated & inspired employees and Marketers, adaptations with the advancement of technology, and last but not least, living in a harmony of peaceful environment.

Our Targets

Our target is to improve the property development line as a valuable platform to shape a powerful group of companies that extends its range of activities both inside and abroad by surviving in all extreme encountered environments. It is motivating to share highlights of our activities as mentioned below:

  1. We are strengthening and enriching a leading position among competitive elite developers in Bangladesh.
  2. Boost our identity as a high-quality and reliable company in all sectors.
  3. Increase our productivity and deliver high-quality customer service through the continuous improvement and development of our staff.
  4. Eliminating the risks and exploiting the opportunities, we will evaluate the market constantly and benefit our clients.
